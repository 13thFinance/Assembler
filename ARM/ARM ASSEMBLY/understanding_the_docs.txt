when readin the ARM DOCS
you might see an instruction being referred to as

intruction Rdest, Rsource1, source2

or in the case of move(mov):

mov Rdest, source2

In these circumstances Rdest and Rsource1 are Registers(ie. r1-r16)

source2 can be a register OR and immediate value(ie. #3 or #4)
source2 can also be other things.
